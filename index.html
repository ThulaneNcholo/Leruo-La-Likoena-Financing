Our Story

Leruo La Likoena Financing

Leruo La Likoena Financing was founded with a simple mission: to help South African businesses thrive by taking the stress out of accounting. After seeing how many entrepreneurs struggled with late submissions, unclear finances, and inconsistent support, we decided to build something better — a service that’s reliable, easy to understand, and fully invested in your success.

We're based in Brakpan, Gauteng, but we serve clients all across South Africa. Whether you’re just starting out or running a growing team, we’ll walk the journey with you — every transaction, tax return, and business decision.
